How to run

Your runner stays at the front of the tab title while the road scrolls left. Jump over ▄, slide under ▀, and keep the action near the front because browser tabs often truncate long titles from the end.

  • Space, W, or ↑ jumps.
  • S or ↓ slides.
  • R restarts after a crash.
  • M toggles sound.
