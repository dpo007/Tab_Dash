# TAB DASH

A tiny, delightfully weird arcade game where your browser tab title becomes the playfield.

You don't need a fancy engine, a giant download, or even a normal screen full of action. Just open `index.html`, focus the page, and try to survive as the road scrolls by in the tab title like a pixel-powered fever dream.

## What is this?

TAB DASH is a one-file HTML game about timing, reflexes, and the strange little magic of browser tab titles. The runner zips along a minimalist track while you:

- jump over low blocks with `Space`, `W`, or `↑`
- slide under high blocks with `S` or `↓`
- restart with `R`
- toggle sound with `M`

The game is simple, snappy, and slightly ridiculous in the best way.

## How to play

1. Open `index.html` in your browser.
2. Click the page or press a control key to start.
3. Keep the tab in focus and react fast.
4. Dodge obstacles, rack up points, and try to beat your best score.

## Game vibes

- Fast-paced arcade action
- Retro, minimal visuals
- Sound effects that make every near-miss feel dramatic
- A proudly odd mechanic: the gameplay is literally displayed in the tab title

## Features

- Score tracking and best-session score
- Jump and slide controls
- Sound on/off toggle
- Crash detection and instant restart
- A tiny glyph legend for the tab-based display

If you like weird little experiments that somehow turn into addictive games, TAB DASH is for you.
